Frequently asked questions

What is your company’s experience?

GH has been operating on the market for over 20 years and is one of Georgia’s leading distributors of international brands.

Which brands do you work with?

GH is the official distributor of Mahmood (rice and tea), Fariman (yeast and flour), and ParsOpal (porcelain dinnerware).

Where is your office located?

Our main office and central warehouse are located in Tbilisi. We deliver to all regions of Georgia.

How can I place an order?

You can place an order through the contact form on our website or by reaching out to our sales team directly.

What are the delivery times?

Tbilisi: 1–3 business days. Regions of Georgia: 3–5 business days. Express delivery is available upon request.

Do you provide product documentation and certificates?

Yes, we provide all necessary accompanying documents, including quality certificates.

Who can become your client?

We primarily work with B2B clients: retail stores, supermarkets, hotels, restaurants, cafes, bakeries, and trading chains.

How can I become a GH partner?

Fill out the B2B inquiry form on our website — our manager will contact you to discuss further details.

Do you offer custom conditions for large clients?

Yes, we provide personalized terms and discounts for wholesale partners.

How can I download your product catalog?

Preview catalogs are available on each brand page. You can request the full version via our B2B form.

What should I do if I face technical issues on the website?

Please email us at support@ghahraman.co ​​ or fill out the contact form. We will respond as soon as possible.
